The No Fun League

Founded in 2013, the No Fun League began as simple snake league amongst a small group of friends. The inaugural draft was conducted live at a local pizza parlor where many of the league managers worked. Over the years, several members have come and gone, but recently the core of the league has stabilized.

In 2019, the league officially converted to auction draft with FAAB waivers and multi-year keepers. Each year, the managers compete to win the championship and avoid the ever-humiliating Toilet Bowl.
