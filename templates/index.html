The No Fun League

Founded in 2013, the No Fun League began as simple snake league amongst a small group of friends. The inaugural draft was conducted live at a local pizza parlor where most of the league members were employed at the time. Over the years, many managers have come and gone, but as of late the core of the league has begun to stabilize.

In the year 2019, the league officially converted to auction draft with FAAB waivers and multi-year keeper eligibility. Some foolish managers choose to allocate a majority of their auction budget towards ultra fragile fantasy positions like running back. Others more tactfully focus their spending on the high floor, high ceiling production found only in quarterbacks and wide receivers.

The competition and comraderie among the constituents of the league has never been feircer, and will only continue to intesify with each passing season. Long live the No Fun League!
